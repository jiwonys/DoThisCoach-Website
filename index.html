What it does

A coaching system for everyday execution.

DoThis is designed around daily follow-through: what to train, what to eat, what to recover, and what to adjust when real life changes the plan.

01

Daily goals

Get a focused plan for the day based on training days, rest days, nutrition targets, and your schedule.

02

Workout generation

Generate strength, sport, warmup, mobility, rehab-aware, and prehab-focused sessions from your goals.

03

Progressive overload memory

Reuse prior workouts, favorite exercises, and training history so sessions can progress more intelligently.

04

Nutrition tracking

Log food, scan barcodes, reuse recent foods, and compare your day against calorie and macro targets.

05

Adaptive coach

Tell DoThis about injuries, soreness, preferences, schedule changes, or goals so future plans can adapt.

06

Workout imports

Bring in your own workout plan, save it, repeat it, or improve it for today's training session.

How it works

From vague goal to useful next move.

DoThis combines structured app data with coaching context. That means your plan can account for your main goal, preferred training split, equipment, food logs, workout history, favorites, and the notes you tell the coach.
