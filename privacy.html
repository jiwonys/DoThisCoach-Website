Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Overview

DoThis helps users manage fitness, nutrition, workout, and coaching information. This policy explains the types of information the app may collect, how it is used, and how to contact us.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, DoThis may collect account information, onboarding answers, goals, workout logs, exercise preferences, food logs, barcode or food search activity, coach messages, subscription status, app diagnostics, bug reports, and support messages.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide daily goals, workout generation, nutrition targets, food logging, coaching guidance, progress history, subscription access, support, safety improvements, product analytics, and bug fixes.

AI Features

Some app features use AI to generate or summarize fitness and nutrition guidance. User-provided context may be sent to AI service providers when needed to provide those features. Avoid entering sensitive medical or personal information that you do not want processed for app functionality.

Subscriptions

Purchases and subscription management are handled by Apple and RevenueCat. DoThis receives subscription status information needed to unlock premium features, but does not store your full payment card details.

Data Retention and Deletion

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the app, maintain records, resolve issues, improve safety, and comply with legal obligations. You can request account deletion or support by emailing help@dothiscoach.com.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to help@dothiscoach.com.
